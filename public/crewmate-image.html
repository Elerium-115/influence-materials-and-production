Crewmate Image Generator

Generate SVG
Download SVG
NOTE: Right-click > "Save Image As..." will NOT produce the same image, as this is a multi-layered SVG.
Then use svgviewer.dev/svg-to-png to convert your SVG into PNG:
  1. Upload your SVG on that page.
  2. Wait around 20 seconds, until the scaled-down image and the "Download PNG" button appears in the bottom-right.
  3. Then click the "Download PNG" button on that page.
NOTE: Other converters may not produce the correct PNG.
Finally, use the resulting PNG to generate your crewmate video on hedra.com
  • TIP: Use elevenlabs.io/text-to-speech for a more customizable audio, that you can import on Hedra.
  • If this is the captain of your crew, you can have the video displayed in-game via the Influence Tools Chrome extension.
